About 10cm studio

✿ stitches with love ✿

Welcome to 10cm studio! We are a team of passionate artist who love nothing more than creating beautiful and handmade creations.

Our journey began last year when our founder, Karry, discovered his passion for art. Since then, he has honed his skills and turned his hobby into a business.

10cm studio aims to provide the best quality handmade clay and crocheted products such as hats, coasters, keychains, plushies, clay trinkets and more, which can be customized and affordable.
